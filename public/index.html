Sangdon Park 박상돈

Assistant Professor

POSTECH AI/CSE
🧭 We develop Trustworthy Cyber-Physical AI.

Sangdon Park is an assistant professor of Graduate School of Artificial Intelligence (GSAI) and Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at POSTECH. He leads Machine Learning Lab in collaboration with other faculty members. He is also a member of National AI Research Lab, Global AI Frontier Lab, and Team Atlanta.

His research focuses on learning for safe and trustworthy cyber-physical AI by developing principled learning algorithms for safe adaptation – from theory to deployment.
