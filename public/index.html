Sangdon Park 박상돈

Assistant Professor

POSTECH GSAI/CSE

Sangdon Park is an assistant professor of Graduate School of Artificial Intelligence (GSAI) and Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at POSTECH. He leads Machine Learning Lab with other faculties. He is also a member of National AI Research Lab, Global AI Frontier Lab, and Team Atlanta.

His research interest lies in Artificial Intelligence (AI) and AI Alignment/Trustworthy AI — designing trustworthy AI systems by understanding from theory to implementation and by considering practical applications in computer security, computer vision, natural language processing, robotics, and cyber-physical systems.
